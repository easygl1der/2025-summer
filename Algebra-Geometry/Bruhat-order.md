> [!thm] Ehresmann criteria
> For $\pi,\sigma\in S_n$, $\pi\leq\sigma$ in Bruhat order iff $\text{rk}(\pi)[i,j]\leq\text{rk}(\sigma)[i,j]$, for any $1\leq i,j\leq n$.
